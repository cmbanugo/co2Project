Recent CO2 Policies:

  • Buenos Aires signed the "Pacto Climatico Global de Ciudades", or the Global Cities Climate Compact, in 2010. The city has set a goal to reduce CO2 emissions by 30%. Read more about Buenos Aires in the Global Covenant of Mayors here.
  • Buenos Aires uses four core pillars to reduce CO2 emissions: waste, transport, energy, and planting trees. Read more about how Buenos Aires reduces CO2 emissions here.
