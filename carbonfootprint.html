Vehicle Travel

Try to walk/ride your bike more often that you drive. If you have to drive try to carpool.

Drive a low carbon vehicle

  • electric cars emit no CO2 if they are charged with clean electricity.

    • Properly inflated tires can improve your mileage by up to 3%

    Avoid traffic by using traffic apps

    Air Travel

    Take fewer and longer vacations that are farther away

    Fly less frequently

    Fly economy class because each flyer splits the value of the carbon emissions

    Home Energy

  • Appliances

    • make energy efficiency a top priority in your home by buying appliances with the ENERGY STAR label

  • Lighting

    • replace incandescent lightbuls with compact fluorescents and or LED bulbs.

  • Thermostat

    • Refrain from using heater or A/C when not necessary and turn these systems off when you leave your home

  • Solar

    • Add solar panels to the roof of your home

    Two companies have reduced upfront costs for this usually pricy option.
